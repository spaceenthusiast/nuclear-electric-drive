Nuclear Electric Drive

Nuclear electric drive combines a nuclear reactor's steady power generation with highly efficient electric thrusters, such as ion or Hall-effect systems, to deliver continuous low-thrust acceleration over long durations. This technology enables faster transit times to Mars and the outer planets, reduced propellant needs, and greater payload capacity compared to traditional chemical rockets.

Ideal for crewed deep space missions and robotic explorers, nuclear electric systems provide reliable power anywhere in the solar system while supporting NASA's vision for sustainable exploration beyond Earth.
